House Business

At The Potter’s House, we take ordinary clay and make functional works of art.

Who We Are

I'm Noel Bradac and I'm a potter with a dream to share unique, functional pottery of high quality. Having earned a BFA in Ceramics from SIU Edwardsville, I am continuing my exploration of clay and its possibilities as a utilitarian fine art.

My beautiful wife Katie and our two wonderful kids live in Sycamore, Illinois. Where the classic charm and small-town living presents an idyllic setting to share our love of art with others in our community.

Our goal is to connect with you through using my pottery. Each handcrafted piece you choose, carries with it warmth and love that we hope is shared with those dear to you.
