Who We Are

My name is Noel Bradac, and I am a potter with a dream to share quality and unique functional pottery with others. I have earned a BFA in ceramics from SIU Edwardsville and wish to continue my exploration of clay and its possibilities as a utilitarian fine art. I am married to my beautiful wife Katie, a teaching artist, and we have 2 wonderful kiddos who keep us on our toes. We live in Sycamore, IL where classic charm and small-town living presents an idyllic setting to share our love of art with others in our community. Our goal is to connect with you through using my pottery. Each handcrafted piece you choose, carries with it warmth and love that we hope is shared with those dear to you.
